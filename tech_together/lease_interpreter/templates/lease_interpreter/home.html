About Just Home

Why we matter

As tenants are usually ignorant of landlord-tenant law and rarely read or take into account the leases they sign, landlords are incentivized to continuously use unenforceable and misleading clauses (UMCs) in their leases.

Harvard Law School conducted a study of 70 leases from the Boston Metropolitan Area. 97 percent of the leases contained at least one unenforceable clause which was illegal.

In addition, in 2016, there were 15,708 evictions in the state of Massachusetts, an average of 43 per day. Just home was created to inform tenants about violations in their leases and to empower them to exercise their rights under Massachussetts law.
